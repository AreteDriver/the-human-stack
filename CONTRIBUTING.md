# Contributing

Thank you for considering a contribution to The Human Stack. This is a living reference, and improvements are welcome.

## What to Contribute

We welcome:

- Evidence upgrades (E2 → E3, E3 → E4, etc.)
- New case studies with measured outcomes
- Benchmark reports with reproducible methodology
- Engineering reviews and postmortems
- Corrections to existing chapters
- Translations (evidence blocks must be preserved)

We do not accept:

- Prompt collections
- Vendor marketing or unverified product claims
- Speculative AGI predictions
- AI news commentary

## How to Contribute

1. **Fork the repository**
2. **Create a branch:** `git checkout -b evidence/upgrade-chapter-name`
3. **Write or modify your chapter** following the templates in `templates/`
4. **Include an evidence block** at the top of every new or significantly modified chapter
5. **Open a pull request** with:
   - Summary of changes
   - Evidence level and confidence for new claims
   - References to any external data or benchmarks

## Review Process

Every contribution is reviewed against [CONSTITUTION.md](CONSTITUTION.md):

1. **Technical Review:** Does the evidence support the claim?
2. **Style Review:** Does it follow [STYLE.md](STYLE.md)?
3. **Evidence Check:** Is the evidence level appropriate?
4. **Merge:** Approved by at least one maintainer

## Templates

Use the templates in `templates/`:

- `case-study.md`
- `engineering-review.md`
- `benchmark-report.md`
- `postmortem.md`
- `architecture-pattern.md`

## Questions?

Open an issue with the label `question` or reach out to the maintainers.
