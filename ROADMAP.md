## Known Gaps

1. **Multi-tenant production evidence.** Most current evidence is from single-node or controlled deployments.
2. **External reproduction.** No chapter has reached E5 yet.
3. **Long-term operational data.** Engineering Reviews need 6+ months of production observation.
